Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens after the 30-day trial?

Your trial automatically converts to the Starter plan unless you upgrade to Professional or Enterprise. You can cancel anytime with no penalties.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! Organizations with 25+ providers qualify for volume pricing. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers. Monthly and annual billing options are available.
